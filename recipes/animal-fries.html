Animal Fries

Description

Here's a recipe for homemade animal fries with In-N-Out style.

Risk your lifespan for deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 32 ounces of frozen french fries
  • 1 teaspoon of distilled white vinegar
  • Salt
  • Two finely diced yellow onions
  • 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp of mustard
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup
  • 6 slices of american cheese
  • 3 tbsp of sweet pickle relish
  • 2/3 cup of mayo

Steps

  1. Add oil to frying pan on medium heat until shimmering
  2. Add onions and salt and cook until onions are soft (up to 10 mins)
  3. Cover pan and stir every few minutes until onions are golden brown (up to 40 mins)
  4. Heat oven to 220 degrees Celsius
  5. Coat two rimmed baking sheets with oil
  6. Stir vinegar, mustard, ketchup, mayo in a bowl
  7. 15 mins after onions are golden brown, add fries to baking sheet and cook in oven until golden brown (up to 25 mins)
  8. Salt fries, add cheese layer, bake until cheese is melted
  9. Add grilled onions to cheese, add sauce, and enjoy!
