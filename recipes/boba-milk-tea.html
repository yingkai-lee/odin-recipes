Boba Milk Tea

Description

Recipe for some sugary and chewy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 10/3 cup water
  • 5/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup tapioca flour
  • 6 black tea bags
  • 3 tbspn half&half
  • 3 tbspn sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Add 1/3 cup water, 1/4 cup sugar to pan over medium heat, cook until sugar dissolves and mixture boils (3-4 mins).
  2. Add pinch of tapioca flour and cook, stir until smooth.
  3. Add half of tapioca flour, sir until sticky dough forms.
  4. Add rest of tapioca flour, stir until dough balls.
  5. Knead dough until flour is completely incorporated and dough is smooth.
  6. Roll dough into long thin ropes, cut rope into 1/4 inch pieces.
  7. Roll each piece into a ball, place in a bowl with tapioca flour and lightly cover with flour.
  8. Bring large pot of water to boil, add pearls to water, reduce heat to medium low, simmer and stir occasionally until cooked (20 mins).
  9. Add 1 cup water and 1 cup brown sugar to small saucepan with medium heat, cook until sugar dissolves (6 mins).
  10. Pour syrup into a heatproof bowl.
  11. Drain pearls, rinse with cold water, add pearls to bowls with syrup and sit for 30-60 mins.
  12. Make the tea in a medium pot over high heat.
  13. Make the milk by whisking together half&half and condensed milk.
  14. Assemble boba milk tea and enjoy!
