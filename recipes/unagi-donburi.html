Unagi Don

Description

This is a delicious recipe for making your own unagi donburi.

The dish should be meaty, succulent, and juicy. When combined with rice, you will have a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 eel fillet with skin
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp mirin
  • 1.5 tbsp cooking wine
  • 200g rice
  • 250 ml water

Steps

  1. Combine mirin and cooking wine in a small saucepan, bring up to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat then add sugar, stir until fully dissolved.
  3. Add soy sauce, bring back up to a boil. Then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Turn off the heat, set aside saucepan to cool.
  5. Cook rice with preferred method.
  6. Preheat grilling unit (maybe oven) to 250 degrees Celsius.
  7. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  8. Add unagi to baking sheet and brush with vegetable oil.
  9. Put baking sheet on grilling unit and grill for 6 minutes.
  10. Remove baking sheet, layer fillets with sauce and return baking sheet to grill until sauce bubbles.
  11. Remove baking sheet, add unagi to rice and add more sauce and enjoy!
